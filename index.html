Physics for Trapped-Ion Quantum Computing

Welcome to the Physics for Trapped-Ion Quantum Computing course! This site provides a clear, textbook-style introduction to the physics specifically for trapped-ion quantum computers.

This course requires familiarity with vectors, complex numbers, linear algebra, and differential equations, however, it may also serve as a refersher course.

We attempt to derive equations more explicitly than a typical textbook to remove potential confusion though some exercises/steps are left to the reader on purpose to encourage engagement and learning.

Exercises contained within will further your understanding of important physics and engineering required of a practitioner of trapped-ion quantum computing. Please use the navigation menu to explore each section.

Questions or concerns can be sent to qarice@ncat.edu
